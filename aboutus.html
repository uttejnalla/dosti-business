About Us

Dosti Business is designed to assist young entrepreneurs in navigating the complexities of starting a business.
The system analyzes user goals, preferences, and scale of business to provide ideas
that align with their interests and financial capabilities.
It offers location-based recommendations, identifying promising areas for launching a business.

The idea for Dosti Business came from a conversation between me and my friend.
We didn't know where this idea would take us, but we decided to start.
Just like in business, taking risks and starting is essential.
Although obstacles may arise, we should keep moving forward,
similar to how friendship begins and continues without going back or leaving.
Our website, Dosti Business, reflects this philosophy of starting strong and persevering,
drawing inspiration from the enduring nature of friendship.
